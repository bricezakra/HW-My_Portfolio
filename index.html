About Me

My name is Brice Zakra. I am a Full Stack Developer with a steady background in Python and some accurate knowledge of the MEARN Stack (MongoDB.js – Express.js – Angular.js – React.js – Node.js) for backend technology. I am currently attending a Full Stack Coding Bootcamp with the University of Texas – Austin to improve my coding skills. I am also a veteran of the US NAVY.

I am an adept of the AGILE values and principles methodology. I have an excellent ability to work well with others and independently as necessary. I have a remarkable analytical and problem solving ability with a strong leadership. You can learn more about me by checking out my Portfolio Page.
